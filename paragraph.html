HTML Paragraphs

The HTML element represents a paragraph. Paragraphs are usually represented in visual media as blocks of text separated from adjacent blocks by blank lines and/or first-line indentation, but HTML paragraphs can be any structural grouping of related content, such as images or form fields.

Paragraphs are block-level elements, and notably will automatically close if another block-level element is parsed before the closing tag.

HTML Display:
With HTML, displaying cannot be changed by adding extra lines or spaces in the codes. The browser will automatically remove these upon display.

How to use Paragraph Element?

Syntax:
<p> (insert text here) </p>

Example:
<p> I am a paragraph. </p>
Result:
I am a paragraph.
